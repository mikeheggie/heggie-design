# Marketer — open questions

Based on `brief.md`, before I can build out the marketing side (paid + SEO + nurture + lead capture) I need to lock down the following.

## Positioning & message

1. **The one-line pitch.** When an agency founder lands on the home page, what's the single sentence they should walk away with? ("Senior design engineer who ships Webflow + Next.js + Sanity work that looks like the agency's own portfolio piece"? Something else?)
2. **Proof points.** What are the 3–5 strongest pieces of evidence we lean on — named past clients/agencies, shipped sites, specific stack wins, recognizable brands? I need the actual list, not placeholders.
3. **Disqualifiers.** Anything you *don't* want to attract — in-house roles, junior titles, non-agency product companies, specific stacks (React Native? WordPress?), under-8-person shops?
4. **Rate / availability framing.** Is this contract, contract-to-hire, or full-time only? Do we mention rate or "let's talk"? Remote-only, or open to on-site / hybrid in specific cities?

## Audience targeting

5. **Geography.** US-only? US + EU? Anywhere? This drives paid targeting and ad spend assumptions.
6. **Agency tier specifics.** "Webflow / Next.js / Sanity-tier shops" — do you have a named target list (e.g. 50 agencies you'd actually want to work at)? If so I want to model the paid channel against that list specifically (LinkedIn ABM, etc.) vs. broad interest targeting.
7. **Buyer vs. influencer.** Is the target the founder/principal, or a design director / hiring lead who'd refer up? Changes the creative tone.

## Paid channel

8. **Budget posture for the mock.** Even though it's mocked, what's the realistic monthly spend we're modeling against — $500, $5k, $25k? Determines whether the plan looks like founder-led LinkedIn outbound + retargeting, or full programmatic.
9. **Channels in scope.** LinkedIn (likely yes), X, Meta retargeting, sponsoring agency-adjacent newsletters (Sidebar, Refind, Designer News), podcasts? Pick the ones to mock end-to-end.
10. **Creative direction.** Should the ad creative show *Mike* (face, voice, personal brand) or the *work* (case-study stills, motion reels)? Or both, split-tested?
11. **Lead magnet.** Is "book a call" the only conversion, or do we also offer a softer asset (teardown of an agency site, "how I'd rebuild your homepage in a week" PDF) for top-of-funnel capture?

# brief

**heggie.design** — a personal site that sells Mike Heggie to agencies as a senior design engineer.

The repo *is* the demo. Everything an agency would scatter across Notion / Figma / Linear / Slack / a code repo lives here in one canvas. Cloning the repo and reading `git log` should walk a hiring manager through how the site was built and why.

---

## Goal

Land Mike a senior frontend / design engineer role at an 8–40 person agency.

**Primary CTA:** Book a call. Every page, post, and surface drives toward it.

---

## Audience

Agency founders and hiring managers at Webflow / Next.js / Sanity-tier shops.

---

## Acquisition (mocked end-to-end)

Two channels, both planned in full:
- **Paid** — aimed at agency founders / hiring managers. Creative concepts, landing variants, lead-capture, CRM hand-off, nurture sequence — all mocked but production-quality.
- **SEO** — foundational posts targeting informational searches from agency operators.

---

## Scope for launch (T+24h)

A polished home page with a book-a-call CTA that delivers leads, three foundational blog posts, and enough surface to demonstrate both the paid and SEO acquisition channels end-to-end.
